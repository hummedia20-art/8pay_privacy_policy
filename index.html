Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 09 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the 8th Pay Calculator app handles user information.

1. About the App

8th Pay Calculator is an independent salary estimation tool created for educational and reference use. It provides 7th CPC salary calculations, projected 8th CPC estimates, DA history and pay-related information.

2. Not a Government App

This app is NOT an official Government of India application and is not affiliated with any ministry, department or commission. All values displayed are for reference only.

3. Official Government Sources Used

The information referenced in this app is derived only from publicly available government data from the following official and accessible websites:

  • Department of Personnel & Training (DOPT)
    https://dopt.gov.in
  • Press Information Bureau (PIB)
    https://pib.gov.in
  • India Code – Government Acts & Notifications
    https://www.indiacode.nic.in
  • National Informatics Centre (NIC)
    https://www.nic.in

4. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No name, phone, email, address, Aadhaar or personal identifiers
  • No account creation or login
  • No salary inputs are stored on our servers

5. Information Collected Automatically

Third-party services may collect non-personal technical data such as:

  • Device model, OS version, app version
  • Crash logs & diagnostics
  • Notification token (OneSignal)
  • Advertising ID (used by Google AdMob)

6. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – Stores & delivers news content
  • OneSignal – Sends push notifications
  • Google AdMob – Displays advertisements
  • Google Play Services – Analytics & diagnostics

7. How Information Is Used

  • To display news and updates
  • To show advertisements
  • To send important alerts
  • To improve app performance

8. Data Security

We use HTTPS for secure communication, but no system is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use means acceptance of the updated policy.
