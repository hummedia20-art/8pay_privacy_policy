Privacy Policy

App: 8th Pay Calculator
Last Updated: 09 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the 8th Pay Calculator app collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. About This App

8th Pay Calculator is an independent salary estimation tool created for educational and reference purposes only. It helps users estimate salary under the 7th Central Pay Commission (CPC) and projected 8th CPC values.

2. NOT A GOVERNMENT APP

This app is NOT an official Government of India application and is NOT affiliated with any government ministry, department, or commission. All salary figures and calculations shown are only for reference and must be verified with official government notifications.

3. Official Government Sources Used

The publicly available government information referenced in this app is taken only from official Government of India websites:

  • Ministry of Finance – https://www.finmin.nic.in
  • Labour Bureau / CPI Index – https://dge.gov.in
  • Department of Personnel & Training – https://dopt.gov.in

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, phone number, email, or Aadhaar collection
  • No login or account creation
  • No salary inputs are stored on our servers

5. Information Collected Automatically

Some technical data may be collected automatically through third-party services:

  • Device type, OS version
  • Crash logs & performance data
  • Notification token (OneSignal)
  • Advertising ID (used by Google AdMob only)

6. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – For storing & serving news content
  • OneSignal – For push notifications
  • Google AdMob – For displaying advertisements
  • Google Play Services – For app analytics & diagnostics

7. How Information Is Used

  • To display news & updates
  • To show advertisements
  • To send important notifications
  • To improve app stability and performance

8. Data Security

We use secure HTTPS connections. However, no digital system is 100% secure.

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children below 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued usage indicates acceptance.
